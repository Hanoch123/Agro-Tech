About Us

This is a web based project which is useful for farmers and agricultural students. This is an open discussion portal providing solutions to farmers and agricultural students. Any general public can also use this system for knowing the information about various crops, and the usage of fertilizers. It provides better solutions for farmers in providing the information regarding soils,crops and fertilizers. It also helps the agricultural students to get practical information regarding various crops. This portal also provides the recommended dose of NPK for various types of crops.
